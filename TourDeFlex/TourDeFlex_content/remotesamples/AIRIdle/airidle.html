The framerate of your application refers to how often the windows of your application are refreshed (the stage is redrawn on screen). With a framerate of 24, the content of your app is refreshed every 41ms (24 times per second). The framerate also defines how often the ENTER_FRAME event handler is invoked. If you build a Flex-based application, the framerate by default will be 24.
